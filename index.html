Biography

Md Mahmudul Hoque is a data science researcher with a passion for uncovering insights from complex datasets. He holds a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from CCN University of Science and Technology in Cumilla, Bangladesh, where he also honed his teaching skills as a Graduate Teaching Assistant.

Mahmudul's dedication to research is evident in his disciplined approach and eagerness to learn. He thrives in collaborative environments and possesses excellent communication skills, allowing him to effectively share his knowledge and contribute to innovative solutions. He is driven by a desire to make a meaningful impact through his research endeavors and is committed to pushing the boundaries of data science.
